CORPORATE DASHBOARD

Uniform communication is one of the biggest challenges large, multi-site companies face. This challenge became more prominent as Maga Design started to expand. To help mitigate the issue, I created this customizable dashboard to help keep Maga Design employees in all four of our studio locations up to date and aware of company events. Using Node/Express, AngularJS, and Google Spreadsheet API, I was able to present announcements and meetings in real-time.

Problem

Internal communication between employees and departments, especially in a multi-site company, is weak.

Solution

Jarvis HD streamlines not only internal communications between employees and departments, but also communicates relevant, engaging and timely information across the Maga-wide organization for all employees, clients, and stakeholders.

Prime Objective

The primary goal is to quickly, yet effectively, provide users with company information they need to be aware of.

My Process

I started off this project by interviewing fellow colleagues at Maga Design to find out what type of information they would like to be regularly updated on. I collected 52 responses using Typeform. This helped me gather a general idea of what company related information employees wanted to know during the work week.

After wireframing and mocking up different design ideas and before moving into development, I displayed the mockup in our front lobby to gain feedback from passerbys. I even sat in the front lobby a few times to observe people's reactions. Majority of the crowd's attention was caught by the dashboard while either coming in or leaving the office. Some would even stop and stare at it for a good five minutes! I received a lot of meaningful feedback, for example the font was too small to read from afar. After making some tweaks I scoped the project for development.
