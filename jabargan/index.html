JAB'ARGAN

My role as the lead designer of Jab'Argan involved creating the brand identity by designing the logo, marketing material, merchant site, packaging and other various touchpoints. I worked with the CEO at the start of the project to define the brand positioning. Because our target market consisted mainly of upper class consumers I focused on designing a brand that looked luxurious and upscale.
