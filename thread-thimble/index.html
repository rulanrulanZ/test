THREAD & THIMBLE

A semester-long project for my corporate design & branding class. We were given a brief overview of a parent clothing brand, Thread and Thimble, and tasked to create the signature, business suite, various touchpoints and a 25+ page graphic standards guide. Most of the work is shown below, omitting the full standards guide. The brand's target market are men and women who are working professionals and are upper middle class. In addition we were tasked to create a sub-brand of Thread and Thimble, Frixon, which you can view on my portfolio.
