National Postal Forum

As part of Maga's involvement for 2016's National Postal Forum (NPF), we were required to create a microsite for the Post Master General to recap the event. My mission was to design and develop the microsite.

You can view the prototype here, and visit the live site here.

Prime Objective

The primary goal of this site is to give users the ability to easily navigate collections of presentations, articles and impressionable quotes from the event.

My Process

Because NPF promotes futuristic thinking and the fact that the site was for the Post Master General, I wanted to design something that was both "futuristic" and "presidential." I was able to better define the user flow as I started sketching wireframes. After collaborating with UX designers on the team to solidify a flow, I expanded on two of my wireframes and designed lo-fi mockups to share with the team. After a few revision cycles, I moved on to decorating!

I created a few emblem type icons and I experimented with bold hues of blue, red, and purple. This pallete helped convey the presidential theme I was aiming for. I used trendy interactions like parallax and animation hovers to convey the futuristic theme. Presidential branding + futuristic transitions...what more could you ask for?
