Deloitte Insight Studio

Deloitte Insight Studio is a one-stop-shop for all things analytics. As the lead UI designer and front end developer for this project, my role involved creating mockups, prototypes, and developing the front end using HTML, CSS, and javascript. In addition, I setup a CMS using Craft for easy editing by the client after handoff.

Prime Objective

The primary goal for this site is to engage prospects by highlighting the studio's offerings and unique process.

My Process

The client wanted the site to have some virtual characteristics to it where you could tour the studio and view all the products. Since the studio did not have any tangible products to show or sell on the site I decided to use pictures of the space to create this virtual feeling. I started with the home page, which shows the front desk and two directional arrows. To comply with the client's request for a virtual site, I added arrows and gave them a hovering transition to suggest a space or "studio" in the site ready for users to tour. Once this method was validated by the client, I continued mocking up additional pages with the same aesthetic. After the mockups were approved, I began development. I started building static pages in HTML for the client to view and then converted them to Craft's templating language for future content editing.
