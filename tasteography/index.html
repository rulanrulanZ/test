TASTEOGRAPHY

A four week project for my course at Thinkful. Tasteography is an application that allows users to find places that serve what they are craving. Imagine being new to an area and all of a sudden have a craving for bubble tea but don't know any places nearby that serves it. Tasteography allows you to search for foods within your area using geolocation and returns a list of establishments that match your craving. As part of my development challenge, my task was to research different APIs and apply one best suited for my application. I chose to use the Google Places API for Tasteography.
